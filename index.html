Introduction

It can be hard to understand why software takes so long to build and deliver. This talk introduces value streams mapping as technique to visualise our money making activities which can highlight waste and allow us to make improvements.

This talk raises 4 key questions...

Question One

How do we make money?

What is it that we do as a business that allows us to make money?

A Value Stream

  1. Something happens!
  2. we respond
  3. we do something else
  4. and another thing
  5. then we collect the money

Lots of different ways of making money, lots of different value streams

Types of Value Streams
